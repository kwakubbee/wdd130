Ghana

Ghana is a country located on the Gulf of Guinea in West Africa. It is relatively small in area and population, but is celebrated for its rich history. The capital of Ghana is the coastal city of Accra. The country is multiethnic, with inhabitants including the Akan, Mossi, Ewe, and Ga-Adangme peoples. Ghana spans diverse ecologies, from coastal savannas to tropical rain forests. It is one of the most stable and democratic countries in West Africa.
