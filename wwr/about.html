About Us --- Happy Riders

Happy Riders, a rafting company that thrives on thrill, nature, and camaraderie: Motto “Ride the Rapids. Feel Alive.” Purpose To ignite the spirit of adventure in every soul by connecting people with the raw beauty and power of rivers. Happy Riders exists to create unforgettable moments through safe, exhilarating rafting experiences that celebrate nature, and teamwork.

History

It all began with a river and a dream. In the summer of 1998, three childhood friends—Kwakubbee, Jake, and Kwame—stood at the edge of the roaring Whitepine River,their paddles in hand and hearts full of wanderlust. They had spent years exploring rivers across continents, from the lush rapids of Ghana’s Volta to the icy flows of the Pacific Northwest. But it was that one unforgettable ride down Whitepine that sparked something bigger: a vision to share the joy of river life with others. With little more than a secondhand raft, a rusty pickup, and a lot of heart, they launched Happy Riders—a name inspired by the laughter and camaraderie they saw on every trip. Word spread quickly. Locals came for the thrill, stayed for the stories, and returned with friends. Over time, Happy Riders grew from a weekend passion into a full-fledged rafting company known for its safety, soul, and signature blend of adventure and hospitality. Today, Happy Riders is more than just a rafting company. It’s a community of explorers, nature-lovers, and thrill-seekers who believe that every river has a story—and every rider deserves a happy one.
